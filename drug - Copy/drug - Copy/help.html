Frequently Asked Questions

The Inventory System in MedEase is a comprehensive tool designed to help you manage all medical supplies, equipment, and other inventory items efficiently. It allows you to track stock levels, monitor expiry dates, and ensure timely reordering to prevent shortages and waste.

You can update inventory by navigating to the "Inventory" section from the dashboard. Here, you can add new items, modify quantities of existing items, or remove items that are no longer in stock. Ensure you have the necessary permissions to make changes.

For further assistance, you can contact our support team through the following methods:

  • Email: support@medease.com
  • Phone: +1-800-MED-EASE
  • Live Chat: Available on the dashboard during business hours.

You can also use the Chat-Bot for quick answers to common questions.
